🎮 WOTC Questline: Youth + Employer Level‑Up Path

Follow the steps below like a story. Each move you make pushes the quest forward. You’re not “just applying” — you’re co‑leading a mission that benefits you and the employer.

01
Player 1 • Youth
Unlock Your Origin Badge

Confirm your foster care history and timing with a caseworker or program — this is your eligibility badge for the WOTC quest.

02
Player 1 • Youth
Collect Your Proof Token

Secure a short verification letter or document that confirms your foster care background — this token activates employer benefits.

03
Player 1 • Youth
Power Up Your Pitch

Practice your value‑focused introduction and mention WOTC as your built‑in bonus perk for employers.

04
Player 1 • Youth
Share Your Quest Guide

Give the employer your one‑pager or link to this site so they understand the WOTC advantage you bring.

05
Co‑Op • Youth + Employer
Co‑Op With Your Employer

Help them complete Forms 8850 and 9061 within 28 days of your start date to activate the WOTC reward.

06
Player 2 • Employer
Start the Pre‑Screen

Employer completes Form 8850 at or before the job offer — this begins their side of the quest.

07
Player 2 • Employer
Complete Character Stats

Employer fills out ETA Form 9061 (or 9062 if you already have a conditional certification).

08
Player 2 • Employer
Submit to TWC

Employer sends the forms to the State Workforce Agency within 28 days to request official WOTC approval.

09
Player 2 • Employer
Receive the Certification Scroll

The State Workforce Agency issues ETA Form 9063 — the official confirmation that you qualify.

10
Player 2 • Employer
Claim the Final Reward

At tax time, the employer uses IRS Form 5884 to claim the WOTC credit — completing the questline.
